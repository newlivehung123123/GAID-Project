# GAID-Project
The Global AI Dataset (GAID) Project is a milestone-based initiative closing existing data and research gaps: Build a web hub for professional AI safety research community, esp. those specialising in AI societal impacts (Phase 0), construct global panel AI data infrastructure to facilitate open-source research (Phases 1-2), and is ever-scaling.

## üöÄ Project Phases

### üìÇ Phase 0: Web Application (AI in Society)
* **Output:** [aiinsocietyhub.com](https://aiinsocietyhub.com/)
* **Function:** Engineered as a non-paywalled platform for data-driven articles on AI societal impacts and a curated AI opportunities board for the professional AI safety research community.

### üìÇ Phase 1: GAID Version 1
* **Output:** [Version 1 Dataset (1998‚Äì2025)](https://doi.org/10.7910/DVN/QYLYSA)
* **Technical Milestone:** Harmonization of three major global AI databases using 123 clinical cleaning and deduplication steps to optimize data integrity by building a dataset to cover global AI data on 214 unique countries and territories across eight domains between 1998 and 2025.
* **Data Sources:** Stanford's AI Index, the Global Index on Responsible AI (GIRAI), and OECD.ai (AI Policy Observatory)

### üìÇ Phase 2: GAID Version 2
* **Output:** [Version 2 Dataset (Expanded)](https://doi.org/10.7910/DVN/PUMGYU)
* **Technical Milestone:** Automated data ingestion, web-scraping and data curation from 11 authoritative sources, expanding the dataset to cover global panel AI data on 227 unique countries and territories across 20 domains between 1998 and 2025.
* **Data Sources:** Stanford's AI Index, the Global Index on Responsible AI (GIRAI), OECD.ai (AI Policy Observatory), MacroPolo Global AI Talent Tracker, UNESCO Global AI Ethics and Governance Observatory, IEA's Energy and AI Observatory, Epoch AI, Tortoise Media - The Global AI Index, WIPO (World Intellectual Property Organisation) - AI Patent Landscapes, Coursera - Global Skills Report (AI & Digital Skills), World Bank - GovTech Maturity Index (GTMI)
